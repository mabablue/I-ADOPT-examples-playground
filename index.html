Ocean - HFR

  • AVRB_QC Average radial bearing quality flag

    QC flags labelling radial velocity of water current measurements with a ‘good_data” flag if the average of the bearings of all the vectors contained in the velocity field lie within the specified range for normal operations. Otherwise, the vectors are labelled with a “bad_data” flag.

  • CCOV Covariance of surface water velocity

    Covariance of the radial current vectors derived from multiple Doppler spectra. It quantifies the statistical consistency and reliability of the measured current vectors across different look directions or time intervals, helping to assess the quality and confidence of the surface current estimates.

  • DDNS_QC Data density quality flag

    QC flags labelling total velocity vectors obtained by the combination of a number of contributing radial vectors bigger than the minimum number defined for normal operations with a “good data” flag. Otherwise, the vectors are labelled with a “bad data” flag.

  • Depth quality flag

    QC flags stating if the depth associated to the data is correct or not.

  • DRVA Direction of radial velocity of water current relative to instrument and to True North by high frequency radar

    The compass direction in degrees positive clockwise from TrueNorth of the component of water velocity in a water body along radial lines centered at the HFR antenna.

  • ESPC Radial velocity (away from) standard deviation over the scatter patch of water current relative to instrument in the water body by high frequency radar

    Standard deviation of the component of water velocity in a water body along radial lines centered at the HFR antenna, evaluated over the scatter patch

  • ETMP Radial velocity (away from) standard deviation of water current relative to instrument in the water body by high frequency radar

    Standard deviation of the component of water velocity in a water body along radial lines centered at the HFR antenna, evaluated over the coverage period

  • EWCS Eastward velocity standard deviation over the coverage period of water current in the water body by high frequency radar

    Standard deviation of multiple measurements taken in a specified time interval (temporal integration time) of the West-to-East component of the velocity of the sea water current on the sea surface (m s-1)

  • EWCT Eastward velocity of water current in the water body by high frequency radar

    Component of the sea surface current velocity that is directed eastward (positive values) or westward (negative values)

  • GDOP Geometric dilution of precision

    Unitless coefficient characterizing the effect that HFR station geometry has on the measurement and position determination errors. A low GDOP corresponds to an optimal geometric configuration of radar stations, and results in accurate surface current data. Essentially, GDOP is a quantitative way to relate the radial and velocity vector uncertainties. Setting a threshold on GDOP for total combination avoids the combination of radials with an intersection angle below a certain value. GDOP is a useful metric for filtering errant velocities due to poor geometry.

  • GDOP threshold quality flag

    QC flags resulting from setting a threshold on GDOP. Setting a threshold on GDOP for total combination avoids the combination of radials with an intersection angle below a certain value.

  • HCSS Radial velocity variance of water current relative to instrument in the water body by high frequency radar

    Variance of the component of water velocity in a water body along radial lines centered at the HFR antenna, evaluated over the coverage period

  • Median filter quality flag

    QC flags labelling radial velocity vectors for which the difference between their module and the median of the modules of all radial vectors lying within a distance of <dLim> km is smaller than the specified threshold for normal operations (curLim), with a "good data" flag. Otherwise, the vector is labelled with a “bad data” flag

  • NCSC Northward velocity standard deviation over the coverage period of water current in the water body by high frequency radar

    Standard deviation of the northward component of water velocity in a water body, evaluated over the coverage period

  • NSCT Northward velocity of water current in the water body by high frequency radar

    Component of the sea surface current velocity that is directed northward (positive values) or southward (negative values)

  • Position quality flag

    QC flags stating if the positions associated to the data is correct or not.

  • QWTR_QC Over-water quality flag

    QC flags labelling radial velocity of water current that lie on water with a good data” flag. Otherwise, the vectors are labelled with a “bad data” flag.

  • RDCT_QC Radial count quality flag

    QC flags labelling radial velocity vectors with a “good data” flag if the number of velocity vectors contained in the velocity field is bigger than the minimum count specified for normal operations. Otherwise, the vectors are labelled with a “bad data” flag

  • RDVA Radial velocity (away from) of water current relative to instrument in the water body by High Frequency Radar

    Component of the sea surface current velocity that is directed toward (negative values) or away from (positive values) the High Frequency Radar (HFR) antenna, measured along the lines of sight of the antenna, i.e. along radial lines centered at the HFR antenna.

  • Time quality flag

    QC flags stating if the timestamp associated to the data is correct or not.

  • UACC Eastward velocity uncertainty of water current in the water body by high frequency radar

    Uncertainty of the component of water velocity in a water body along radial lines centered at the HFR antenna.

  • UACC Eastward velocity uncertainty of water current in the water body by high frequency radar

    Uncertainty of the eastward component of water velocity in a water body.

  • UVAD Eastward velocity average in the water by a moored acoustic doppler current profile (ADCP)

    UVAD (m s-1) - ADCP datafile

  • VACC Northward velocity uncertainty of water current in the water body by high frequency radar

    Uncertainty of the northward component of water velocity in a water body.

  • VART_QC Variance quality flag

    QC flags labelling surface velocity vectors whose temporal variance is smaller than a maximum variance threshold with a “good data” flag. Otherwise, the vectors are labelled with a “bad data” flag. This test is applicable only to Beam Forming (BF) systems. Data files from Direction Finding (DF) systems will apply instead the “Temporal Derivative” test

  • Velocity quality flag

    QC flags labelling surface velocity vectors whose module is smaller than a maximum velocity threshold with a “good data” flag. Otherwise, the vectors are labelled with a “bad data” flag.

  • Atmospheric boundary layer height defined by temperature inversion

  • Biological sex

    Biological sex [female | male | intersex | unknown]

  • Cloud cover

    The amount of sky estimated to be covered by all cloud types (cloud amount). The estimate is made to the nearest okta (eighth) and is reported on a scale which is essentially one of the nearest eighth, except that figures 0 and 8 on the scale signify a completely clear and cloudy sky, respectively, with consequent adjustment to the adjacent 1 and 7 okta intervals.

  • Coarse woody debris abundance

    CWD abundance per hectare (m2/hectare) is the abundance of CWD measured from all the plots/transects scaled to a hectare (100 x 100 m). They are calculated using the formula (see https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S037811270900783X)

  • Concentration of DDT impacted by industry

    Concentration of DDT in a given year at a site impacted by industry.

  • Current smoking status

    Smoking status current of a patient

  • Date of last PCR-test

    date of last COVID-19 PCR test performed by a certified laboratory

  • Distance to nearest neighbour habitat patch

    This variable is part of the EBV Connectivity of terrestrial ecosystem habitat types and helps to measure the degree of connection of EUNIS habitats within a landscape, in terms of their spatial distribution. https://github.com/EuropaBON/EBV-Descriptions/wiki/Terrestrial-Connectivity-of-terrestrial-ecosystem-habitat-types

  • Docosahexaenoic acid content per dry weight (DHA content/ C22:6 n-3 content)

    The amount of docosahexaenoic acid relative to dry weight in an individual.

  • dose of applied nanomaterial internalised by a specific cell population (or per single cell) - particle number / cell area

    Correlation of dose and response for nanomaterials toxicity assessment requires information on the amount of the applied dose of nanomaterial that has been internalised by a cell population (or per specific cell) and is dependent on a number of factors including the exposure time, the particle density (i.e., whether settling occurs and thus the distribution of particles in the medium is non-homogeneous affecting the local concentration at the cell surface) etc.

  • dose of applied nanomaterial internalised by a specific cell population (or per single cell) -mass per cell area

    Correlation of dose and response for nanomaterials toxicity assessment requires information on the amount of the applied dose of nanomaterial that has been internalised by a cell population (or per specific cell) and is dependent on a number of factors including the exposure time, the particle density (i.e., whether settling occurs and thus the distribution of particles in the medium is non-homogeneous affecting the local concentration at the cell surface) etc.

  • Dynamic shear viscosity of polystyrene PS042

    Dynamic shear viscosity of polystyrene PS042 under the testing conditions of DIN 51810-1.

  • Electron density in the solar wind

    Density (particle per cm3) of electrons measured in the Solar Wind.

  • Feral-free enclosure area

    The total area (in ha) of feral-free enclosures. See https://linked.data.gov.au/def/nrm/d936bfd5-d2f9-5107-bbf7-2bd18bbab42b

  • Foliage projective cover in the lower canopy strata

    The proportion of the ground area covered by foliage (or photosynthetic tissue) held in a vertical plane. Foliage Projective Cover is usually expressed as the percentage of ground covered by foliage and can be separated according to vegetation strata.

  • Foraminifera, planktic, size

    Size of planktic foraminifers in surface sediments, see https://doi.pangaea.de/10.1594/PANGAEA.126730

  • Mass concentration of isobutylene in chloroform

    Mass concentration of isobutylene in chloroform.

  • Mass flux of carbon into soil from vegetation due to senescence

    In accordance with common usage in geophysical disciplines, flux implies per unit area, called flux density in physics. Vegetation means any living plants e.g. trees, shrubs, grass. The specification of a physical process by the phrase due_to_ process means that the quantity named is a single term in a sum of terms which together compose the general quantity named by omitting the phrase. The term senescence means loss of living biomass excluding plant death, e.g. leaf drop and other seasonal effects. The term refers to changes in the whole plant and is not confined only to leaf drop.

  • Maximum trunk char height

    Trunk char height is a measure of the extent of fire height above ground which is measured from the base to the highest point of fire occurrence on the tree trunk. Maximum char height is measured at the community level using a 360° sweep at each sampling location.

  • Nervosity feelings

    In the past 30 days, how often did you feel nervous?

  • Overnight stays in 3-star hotel near the sea shore

    Number of nights in a 3-star hotel near the sea shore

  • Peak ground acceleration

    Peak acceleration measured on the earth surface when facing seismic events, like earthquakes.

  • Probablity of occurence of habitat

    Part of terrestrial Ecosystem distribution of terrestrial EUNIS Habitats EBV, probability of occurrence of EUNIS habitats within contiguous spatial units (grid cells of 100x100 m) across the EU over 1 year time. https://github.com/EuropaBON/EBV-Descriptions/wiki/Terrestrial-Ecosystem-distribution-of-terrestrial-EUNIS-Habitats

  • Resting systolic blood pressure

    Resting blood pressure systolic in millimeters of mercury (mmHg)

  • Sheet resistance of layer of gold

    The sheet resistance of a 5-nanometer-thick layer of gold.

  • Standard metabolic rate in mg of Oxygen per hour

    The measure of an ectotherm animal's baseline metabolic rate (i.e. non-active, non-stressed) measured by the milligrams of oxygen consumed in a period of time of one hour.

  • Strike of bedding

    Geographic azimuth (relative to true north) at a point observation location, of a horizontal line contained in a sedimentary rock bedding surface. The bedding surface must not be horizontal; the azimuth is reported such that the dip direction of the inclined bedding is to the right when facing in the azimuth direction.

  • Weight specific-ingestion Carbon rate at 15 °C

    The amount of carbon consumed by an organism at non-limiting concentration of food relative to the individual dry weight measured at 15°C. It is expressed as μg C mg DW−1 h−1
